1. What IconDock is

IconDock runs inside the Webflow Designer and may communicate with servers we operate (for example, to search icons and serve SVG or image data). If you enable features that use Webflow’s OAuth or Data API, Webflow’s processing also applies; see Webflow’s privacy documentation for how Webflow handles account data.

2. Information we may process

Depending on how you use the Service, we may process:

We do not sell your personal information.

Our servers and vendors (for example, cloud hosting) may be located in the United States or other countries. By using the Service, you understand that information may be transferred to jurisdictions with different data protection laws.

4. Retention

We retain information only as long as needed for the purposes above, unless a longer period is required by law. Server logs may be kept for a limited period for security and troubleshooting.

5. Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

6. Your choices

You may disconnect OAuth integrations in Webflow where applicable, stop using the extension, or contact us with privacy requests as described below. Rights vary by region (for example, GDPR or CCPA); we will respond in line with applicable law.

7. Children

The Service is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their personal information.
